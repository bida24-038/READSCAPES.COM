Our Mission

Why we do what we do

At READSCAPES, our mission is simple yet profound: we want to ignite the love for reading in every individual. Whether you're discovering a book for the first time or diving into a beloved classic, we aim to be your trusted companion on this literary journey.

By offering a curated library, insightful reviews, and a thriving community, we empower you to explore the endless worlds within the pages of books.

Our Story

READSCAPES was founded in 2020 by a group of book enthusiasts who wanted to create a space where readers could connect over their shared love of literature.

What began as a small online book club quickly grew into a thriving platform with thousands of members from around the world. Today, we're proud to offer not just a place to discover books, but a true community for readers of all kinds.

Our journey has been filled with wonderful stories - both the ones we've read and the ones we've created together with our members.
