Smart Bookmarks

Our Smart Bookmarks feature revolutionizes how you save and revisit important passages in your books. Unlike traditional bookmarks that simply mark a page, our system allows you to:

  • Tag bookmarks with custom categories (quotes, research, favorite scenes)
  • Color-code for quick visual reference
  • Add personal notes to each bookmark
  • Search across all your bookmarks
  • Export to create personal quote collections
See Tutorial

Advanced Annotation Tools

Transform how you interact with texts using our comprehensive annotation system:

  • Multiple highlight colors for different purposes
  • Margin notes that stay with the text
  • Link related annotations across books
  • Create study guides from your annotations
  • Share annotations with study groups
See Tutorial
